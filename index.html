PTJ – Pursue The Journey – was created by Lewis Tye, Noah Pearson, and Cody Jeacock. Inspired by a passion for fitness, discipline, and personal growth, they wanted to build more than just a gym. PTJ is a community where everyone can push their limits, grow stronger, and stay motivated every step of the journey.
